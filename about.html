Our Story

For all the ladies and gentlemen, for the ladies with gentles hands and most importantly, for all the dogs, we present to you The Drake Store.

The Drake Store was founded in 2025 by a collective of OVO fans who believed the movement deserved a centralized hub for all authentic merchandise and memorabilia. We started with a small collection of vintage CDs and quickly grew into the premier destination for exclusive drops and limited edition releases.

Our Mission

To provide fans worldwide with authentic, high-quality merchandise that celebrates the music, legacy, and culture of the 6 God, ensuring every product is 'Certified Lover Boy'-approved.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and recognized global hub for authentic, exclusive, and culturally relevant music and lifestyle apparel.

Our Values

  • Outstanding Quality: We focus relentlessly on premium fabrics and impeccable craftsmanship, ensuring every piece reflects luxury and lasting construction.
  • Verified Exclusivity: We guarantee access to authentic, limited-edition merchandise and rare drops, protecting the value and integrity of the OVO legacy.
  • Originality in Culture: We drive culture, not follow it. Our collection represents the leading edge of music fashion and lifestyle influence.
